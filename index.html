DEPARTMENT OF COMPUTER SCIENCE

Instituted in the year 1987, the Department of Computer Science is a research oriented department that specializes in futuristic concepts like Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics. The department has well experienced faculties with a combined experience of more than half a century. The active research areas of the department are Algorithms and Artificial Systems, Computer Graphics, Digital Image Processing, Neural Networks, Data Mining and Big data Analytics. The department offers postgraduate courses of Masters in Computer Applications, Masters in Computer Science, research courses of Masters in Philosophy and doctorate in philosophy. The admission for MCA course is based on common entrance test in the state of Tamilnadu(TANCET).

    III SEMESTER : Machine Learning, Digital Image Processing, Data Analytics, Machine Learning and Image Processing lab, Internship-I.
    IV SEMESTER : Project Work.

About Us

The University was established on 5th September 1857. The University was organised in the model of London University.Madras University is the mother of almost all the old Universities of southern India. The University imparts both Under Graduate and Post Graduate Education through the Affiliated Institutions which are spread over the districts of Chennai, Thiruvallur and Kancheepuram. Apart from teaching, research activities in Arts, Humanities, Science, Management and Technology are the main portals at the University.

A number of Institutions affiliated to Madras University Concentrate in research activities where Ph.D., Programme is available in their respective field of specialisation. The University is also offering teaching and Research programmes in 4 Campuses of Madras University. At present there are 70 University Departments of study and research are spread over in 4 Campuses organised into 17 Schools each of which offer Post Graduate Courses in respective specialisation, part time and full time Ph.D. Programmes, Diploma and Certificate Programmes.

The University has been accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with the five stars rating first, and later with an A Grade rating. The UGC has recognised the University as one of the centres for potential for excellence in the country.
